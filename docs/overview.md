## ğŸ® Gameplay Features

### Weapon Cleaning
Players can clean their weapons using consumable cleaning items (e.g., weapon cloth). The cleaning process includes:
- Animation with cloth prop
- Camera zoom and rotation around the weapon
- Weapon dirt and degradation reset
- Notification on completion

### Weapon Inspection
Players can inspect their weapons to view stats and condition:
- Press Middle Mouse Button (MMB) while in weapon wheel
- Use `/inspect` command
- View weapon degradation, dirt, and stats
- Immersive inspection animation

### Admin Commands
- `/cleanweap` - Clean equipped weapon without using item
- `/inspect` - Inspect equipped weapon
